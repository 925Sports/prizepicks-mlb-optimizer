How to Use PrizePicks NBA Optimizer

This tool helps you optimize your NBA bets on PrizePicks.

  • Use the search bar to find players or stats.
  • Filter by games, teams, callouts, bet tags, fav status, or game scoring using the buttons.
  • Select odds type: All, Standard, Demon, or Goblin.
  • Switch tabs to view different stat categories.
  • On each player card, click 'More' or 'Less' to add bets to your slip.
  • View and filter generated bet slips with calculated edges and payouts.
  • Click the info icon on cards for detailed player info, game logs, splits, etc. (matches the Tatum Rebounds popup style).

How to Use PrizePicks MLB Optimizer

This tool helps you optimize your MLB bets on PrizePicks.

  • Use the search bar to find players or stats.
  • Filter by games, teams, or callout tags using the buttons.
  • Select odds type: All, Standard, Demon, or Goblin.
  • Only stat types with available data are active (others are greyed out).
  • Click 'More' or 'Less' on cards to add to bet slip.
  • View generated bet slips with edge and payout calculations.
